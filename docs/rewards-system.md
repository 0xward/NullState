# Rewards System

## 💰 How to Earn USDm

NullState offers multiple income streams:

1. **Burn Items** (Weekly) - Convert loot to USDm
2. **Vault Quest** (Weekly) - Solve code puzzle
3. **Seasonal Bonuses** (Monthly) - Rank in leaderboard

---

## 🔥 Burning Items

### What is "Burning"?

**Burning** = Converting your loot items into USDm stablecoins directly to your wallet.

- Items are **permanently removed** from inventory
- Same as trading them for cash
- Quick, simple, no middleman

### What Can Be Burned?

✅ **Burnables:**
- Uncommon items
- Rare items  
- Epic items
- Legendary items

❌ **Cannot Burn:**
- Common items (too common)
- Paper (quest item)
- Golden Key (quest item)

### How to Burn

1. Open **Inventory**
2. Select items to burn (multi-select ok)
3. Click **"Burn Items"** → "Convert to USDm"
4. Confirm transaction
5. Items deleted, USDm added to burn pool
6. Claim anytime via **"Claim Weekly Rewards"**

### Burn Values (Random)

| Rarity | Value Range | Probability |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Uncommon | 0.01 - 0.05 | 30% drop rate |
| Rare | 0.05 - 0.2 | 20% drop rate |
| Epic | 0.2 - 0.5 | 8% drop rate |
| Legendary | 0.5 - 1.0 | 2% drop rate |

Each item's burn value is **randomized within its range**. Higher rarity = more valuable!

### Weekly Burn Cap

- **Pool**: 2 USDm per week (owner can increase)
- **Per-User Cap**: 0.5 USDm max/week (flexible)
- **How it works**:
  - Burn as much as you want
  - Claim anytime during the week
  - Capped at 0.5 USDm per user
  - If pool depletes early → pro-rata distribution

**Example:**
```
Week 1: Owner deposits 2 USDm
- You burn 5 items worth 0.8 USDm
- You can claim: 0.5 USDm (capped)
- Remaining: 0.3 USDm rolls to next week

Week 2:
- Owner deposits 2 USDm
- Your rollover: 0.3 USDm + new cap: 0.5 USDm = 0.8 USDm available
- You claim: 0.8 USDm
```

### Claiming Rewards

- **Timing**: Claim anytime during the week (Monday 00:00 - Sunday 23:59 UTC)
- **Frequency**: Multiple claims ok (if you burn again)
- **Reset**: Every Monday 00:00 UTC (ISO week)
- **To Claim**: Inventory → "Claim Weekly Rewards" → Approve transaction

## 🏆 Seasonal Bonus Rewards

### Leaderboard Rankings

Each month, the top 3 players by **combined score** earn bonus USDm:

| Rank | Reward | Distribution |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 🥇 1st | 20 USDm | Instant transfer |
| 🥈 2nd | 5 USDm | Instant transfer |
| 🥉 3rd | 3 USDm | Instant transfer |

### How Rankings Work

Your seasonal score is calculated from:

- **45%** - Total USDm Earned (burns + bonuses)
- **20%** - Total Items Collected
- **15%** - Enemies Killed
- **15%** - Days Active (loyalty)
- **5%** - Vault Codes Solved (bonus)

**Example Leaderboard:**
```
Rank 1: Alice
  - USDm Earned: 8.5 (45%)
  - Items: 450 (20%)
  - Kills: 8,000 (15%)
  - Days Active: 25 (15%)
  - Vault: 4 correct (5%)
  - Score: 87.3 / 100
  - Reward: 20 USDm ✅

Rank 2: Bob
  - USDm Earned: 7.2
  - Items: 420
  - Kills: 7,500
  - Days Active: 22
  - Vault: 3 correct
  - Score: 81.1 / 100
  - Reward: 5 USDm ✅

Rank 3: Carol
  - ... (Score: 78.5 / 100)
  - Reward: 3 USDm ✅
```

### When to Claim

- **Season ends**: Last day of UTC month (23:59)
- **Claim window**: 7 days after season end
- **After owner deposits**: Requires owner to deposit bonus pool
- **Instant payout**: Click "Claim Season Bonus" → USDm transfers

## 📊 Total Earning Potential

### Per Month

```
If you:
- Burn items weekly (0.5 × 4 weeks) = 2.0 USDm
- Solve vault code weekly (1 × 4 weeks) = 4.0 USDm
- Rank 3 in leaderboard = 3.0 USDm

Total: ~9 USDm/month

If you rank 1: +17 more USDm = 26 USDm total!
```

### Earning Tips

1. **Burn uncommon+ items** - Only these earn USDm
2. **Collect diverse rarities** - Legendary items = bigger burns
3. **Solve vault weekly** - Guaranteed 1 USDm/week
4. **Stay active daily** - Days Active = leaderboard points
5. **Kill everything** - Combat engagement = leaderboard edge
6. **Build consistent record** - No reset, stats carry over

## 🪙 About USDm

**USDm** (Mento Dollar) is a stablecoin on Celo. 

- **Value**: 1 USDm ≈ $1 USD
- **Backed by**: Mento protocol
- **Network**: Celo Mainnet
- **Wallet**: Transfer to any Celo-compatible wallet
- **Exchange**: Trade on DEX (Uniswap, etc.)

Your USDm earnings are **real, on-chain value**.
