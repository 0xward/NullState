# Frequently Asked Questions

## 🎮 Gameplay

**Q: How long is each game session?**
A: As short or long as you like — runs are floor-by-floor and you can Save & Exit anytime.

**Q: Do I lose items if I die?**
A: No. Items stay in inventory. You respawn on the same floor with full HP.

**Q: What's permadeath?**
A: Softened. Dying doesn't end your run or send you back to floor 1 — you respawn on the same floor with full HP. Loot you haven't burned carries with you across bunkers until you spend it.

**Q: Are there classes?**
A: No class picks — you play the Knight. Your power and look come from the gear you equip (weapons, armor, cosmetic outfits).

**Q: How many bunkers are there?**
A: 5 bunkers (story Acts) × 5 floors = 25 depths.

**Q: Is there anything after I finish all 5 bunkers?**
A: Yes — two modes unlock on the title screen. **New Game+**: replay from Act 1 at a higher Cycle where enemies are +35% tougher per Cycle and Glitch Shards drop +25% richer, carrying your gear and level. **The Null Abyss**: an endless descent below Bunker 5 — go as deep as you can; your deepest floor is your season rank, the deep floors drop the best (T3) shards, and death ends the dive.

**Q: How does the Null Abyss leaderboard work?**
A: Your deepest floor reached in the Abyss this season is your score. The top 3 divers at season end split the real stablecoin season bonus (claiming needs that season's Pass, same as the leaderboard bonus).

**Q: What is the Armory Trial?**
A: A one-time reward for clearing Act 1 — pick any 2 premium weapons in the Marketplace and use them free for 48 hours each. Each weapon's timer starts the first time you equip it; when it expires the weapon returns to the shop.

**Q: How do referrals work?**
A: Share your code from the Referral menu. A friend counts once they clear Act 1 using your link. Rewards: 1 friend = a 3-day weapon trial; 3 friends = a permanent skin + three 48h weapon trials; 10 friends = two 7-day weapon trials. Bonus: your invitee's first weapon purchase or pass mint automatically mints a free Season Pass to your wallet.

## 💰 Rewards & Earning

**Q: What do I get from burning items?**
A: NullState Point — an in-game, faucet-only currency credited instantly. It is NOT real money and NOT withdrawable; spend it on lower-priced Marketplace gear via Swap.

**Q: How do I earn real stablecoin?**
A: Two ways: solve the weekly Treasure Vault code (find the Golden Key + Code Paper, then enter the code at the Vault door), and finish top-3 on the seasonal leaderboard while holding that season's Pass.

**Q: Does owning a pass change drop rates?**
A: No. Drop rates are identical with or without a pass. The pass gives the exclusive Warden outfit, daily perks, and the right to claim a top-3 season bonus.

**Q: Do I pay gas fees?**
A: Celo fees are sub-cent, and the game defaults gas to whichever stablecoin you hold the most of (fee abstraction) — you don't need to hold CELO.

**Q: Can I cash out stablecoin I earn?**
A: Yes — vault and season payouts land directly in your wallet as USDT. It's yours; spend or transfer it like any MiniPay balance.

## 🔐 Vault Quest

**Q: Where exactly are the Paper and the Golden Key?**
A: We won't tell! They drop from the rarer interactive containers as you play — each capped at one per wallet per week.

**Q: What if I don't find them this week?**
A: The code, your Golden Key, and your Paper allowance all reset every Monday 00:00 UTC. Try again next week.

**Q: Is the vault code mine alone?**
A: There is one shared code per week, but reading it requires your own Paper — and submitting requires your own Paper AND Golden Key, so the code alone unlocks nothing.

**Q: What if I forget the code?**
A: Re-check your Paper item (inventory → view details) — it always shows the current week's code.

## 🏆 Leaderboard

**Q: How is the leaderboard ranked?**
A: By experience earned in your runs, per season (monthly). It updates as you play.

**Q: What if I'm rank 4 at season end?**
A: No bonus — only the top 3 are paid. New season, fresh start.

**Q: What stops cheating?**
A: All reward-bearing actions (burns, claims, vault submissions) are validated server-side, and payouts are on-chain and publicly auditable.

## 🎫 Pass System

**Q: Is the pass really non-transferable?**
A: Yes, it's a Soulbound NFT — locked to your wallet.

**Q: What happens at season end?**
A: The pass expires (perks stop) but stays in your wallet — and it still counts for claiming that season's top-3 bonus.

**Q: Can I get refunds?**
A: No, all mints are final.
