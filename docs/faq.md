# Frequently Asked Questions

## 🎮 Gameplay

**Q: How long is each game session?**  
A: 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on how deep you go. You can save anytime.

**Q: Can I pause the game?**  
A: Yes, click "Save Game" to pause. Progress is locked.

**Q: Do I lose items if I die?**  
A: No. Items stay in inventory. You respawn on the same floor with full HP.

**Q: What's permadeath?**  
A: Softened. Dying doesn't end your run or send you back to floor 1 — you respawn on the same floor with full HP. Loot you haven't burned carries with you across bunkers until you spend it.

**Q: Can I change classes mid-game?**  
A: No, pick at start. One class per run.

**Q: How many bunkers can I complete in one session?**  
A: All 6 if you grind. Most players do 2-3 per session.

## 💰 Rewards & Earning

**Q: How do I get my first USDm?**  
A: Burn any uncommon+ item. Backend records → You claim weekly.

**Q: Does owning a pass guarantee more drops?**  
A: No, just better drop rates. You might still get common items, just less often.

**Q: Can I earn USDm without buying pass?**  
A: Yes! Vault codes (1/week) + vault rewards (5/month) work without pass.

**Q: What's the fastest way to earn?**  
A: Buy pass → farm epics/legendaries in Bunker 5 → burn for max value.

**Q: Do I pay gas fees?**  
A: No, Celo is cheap. Maybe 0.01 CELO per transaction (~$0.001).

**Q: Can I cash out my USDm?**  
A: Yes, send to exchange (Kraken, Coinbase, etc.) and convert to fiat.

**Q: Can I link multiple wallets to one account?**
