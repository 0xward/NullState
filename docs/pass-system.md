# Pass System

## 🎫 What is a Season Pass?

A **Season Pass** is a Soulbound NFT (non-transferable) that grants you:

- ✅ Unlimited gameplay (per season)
- ✅ Normal item drop rates
- ✅ Full access to all bunkers
- ✅ One pass per season (monthly)

## 💳 Two Ways to Play

### Option 1: With Pass (Premium) 🌟

**Cost**: 0.3 USDm (one-time per season)

**Benefits**:
- Unlimited plays during season
- Normal item drop rates
- Standard legendary access
- Full leaderboard eligibility

**How to Get**:
1. Click "Buy Pass" in main menu
2. Approve 0.3 USDm payment
3. Receive Soulbound NFT
4. Start playing unlimited

### Option 2: Without Pass (Free) 🆓

**Cost**: Free

**Benefits**:
- Unlimited plays (no hard limit)
- Reduced drop rates (fewer epics/legendics)
- Still earn USDm from burns
- Can compete in leaderboard

**Drop Rate Difference:**

| Rarity | With Pass | Without Pass |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| Common | 40% | 70% |
| Uncommon | 30% | 25% |
| Rare | 20% | 4% |
| Epic | 8% | 1% |
| Legendary | 2% | 0% |

**In plain English:**
- Without pass: You get mostly common/uncommon items
- With pass: Equal chance at all rarities
- **Result**: Pass-holders earn more USDm per run

## 🆓 Free FCFS Passes (Limited)

### What is FCFS?

**FCFS** = "First Come, First Served"

For each season, the first **50 players** get a free pass:

1. Be in the first 50 to mint
2. Get free pass (value 0.3 USDm)
3. No payment needed
4. Soulbound NFT to your wallet

**Availability**: Limited per season, so early players get advantage!

## 🔒 Pass Verification

**How does the game know you have a pass?**

1. Your wallet address is checked
2. Smart contract verifies: do you own a Season Pass NFT?
3. If YES → Unlock normal drop rates
4. If NO → Unlock reduced drop rates

**No additional login needed.** It's automatic based on your wallet.

## 🚫 Pass FAQs

**Q: Can I transfer my pass to a friend?**  
A: No. Passes are **Soulbound** - locked to your wallet forever. Can't trade, sell, or gift.

**Q: What happens when season ends?**  
A: Your pass expires. Buy next season's pass for continued premium play.

**Q: Can I refund my pass?**  
A: No refunds. 0.3 USDm is non-refundable once minted.

**Q: Do I keep pass NFT after season?**  
A: Yes, it stays in your wallet as a collectible/proof. But it won't grant play benefits in next season.

**Q: Can I buy multiple passes?**  
A: No, only 1 per user per season. System prevents double-minting.

**Q: What if I didn't get a FCFS pass?**  
A: Buy one for 0.3 USDm, or play free with reduced drop rates.
