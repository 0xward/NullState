# Pass System

## 🎫 What is a Season Pass?

A **Season Pass** is a Soulbound NFT (non-transferable) for the current
season. Holding an active-season pass grants:

- ✅ The exclusive **Warden** outfit (cosmetic only — zero stats)
- ✅ A profile / in-game **badge**
- ✅ **Daily perks**, claimable once per UTC day: +1 bonus energy run **or**
  a small Glitch-Shard stipend (3× T1)
- ✅ Eligibility to **claim the seasonal top-3 leaderboard bonus** (real
  stablecoin — ranking top-3 without a pass for that season cannot claim)

Deliberately **non-pay-to-win**: item drop rates are IDENTICAL with or
without a pass, the HP cap is untouched, and everything a pass holder can
farm, a free player can farm too — the perks are convenience and cosmetics,
plus the season-bonus claim right.

---

## 📅 Season Structure

Each season runs for **1 calendar month (UTC)**, July–December 2026
(6 seasons pre-initialized on-chain). Reset: 00:00 UTC on the 1st.

✅ **Resets:** leaderboard rankings, seasonal stats, weekly vault codes
❌ **Does NOT reset:** your items/stats, account data, wallet balances,
pass history (expired passes stay in your wallet as collectibles)

---

## 💳 Two Ways to Play

### With Pass 🌟
- Price is set **on-chain** (`PassSBTv3.passPriceUsdCents`) and shown live
  on the Season Pass screen; payable in USDm, USDC, or USDT.
- Grants the perks listed above for that season only.

### Without Pass (Free) 🆓
- Play, loot, burn, craft, and compete on the leaderboard — all free.
- Same drop rates as pass holders.
- The only things you miss: the Warden outfit, the daily perk claims, and
  the right to claim a top-3 season bonus.

---

## 🆓 Free Passes (Whitelist / FCFS)

Each season, a limited number of free passes can be granted via the
on-chain whitelist (`mintFreePass`) — first come, first served for
whitelisted wallets. Follow the community channels to get whitelisted.

---

## 🔒 Pass Verification

1. Your wallet address is checked against the PassSBTv3 contract on-chain.
2. Holding a pass for the CURRENT season unlocks the perks automatically.
3. No extra login — it's your wallet.

Season-bonus claims check the pass **for the specific season being
claimed**, so you can still claim a past season's bonus after it ends, as
long as you held that season's pass.

---

## 🚫 Pass FAQs

**Q: Can I transfer my pass?**
A: No. Soulbound — locked to your wallet. Can't trade, sell, or gift.

**Q: What happens when the season ends?**
A: The pass expires (perks stop). It stays in your wallet as proof — and it
still counts for claiming that season's top-3 bonus.

**Q: Can I refund a pass?**
A: No refunds once minted.

**Q: Can I buy multiple passes?**
A: One per wallet per season — double-minting is blocked on-chain.

**Q: Does a pass make me stronger?**
A: No. Drop rates and combat are identical. It gives cosmetics,
convenience perks, and the season-bonus claim right.
